Bánh Xèo

Description

A favourite dish of mine. It is always a pleasure to have Bánh Xèo and I often recommend it to those that are trying Vietnamese cuisine (you don't have to always get the Pho!)

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • All-purpose flour
  • Tumeric
  • Filtered Water
  • Coconut Cream
  • Salt
  • Green onions
  • Dry mung beans
  • Pork belly
  • Medium yellow onion
  • Bean sprouts
  • Head mustard greens
  • Cilantro
  • Vienamese perilla

Steps

  1. Prepare the batter by combining rice flour, all-purpose flour, tumeric, filtered water, coconut cream, and salt.
  2. Soak the mung beans until they become soft.
  3. Cook the pork belly and slice thinly.
  4. Prepare the shrimp.
  5. Wash vegetables and greens.
  6. Sauté some onions, pork, and shrimp.
  7. Pour in and spread te batter evenly.
  8. Add mung beans, bean sprouts, and cover with lid for 3 minutes.
  9. Remove lid and let crepe become crisp.
