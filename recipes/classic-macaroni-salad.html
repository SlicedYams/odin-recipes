Classic Macaroni Salad

Description

This five ingredient recipe is simple, quick, and is an easy introduction to cooking. Total preparation and cook time is only 30 minutes so you can spend more of your time thinking about what to cook next!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Mayonnaise
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustad, salt, pepper, and cooked macaroni to taste.
  3. Mix in any additional ingredients.
  4. Chill in fridge for four hours.
