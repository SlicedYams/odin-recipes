Uncrustable

Description

If you thought macaroni salad was easy, get ready for an awesome take on an old classic. The uncrustable is an enclosed peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Pinched on the edges with the crust cut off, the uncrustable is a delicious pocket of sweetness!

Ingredients

  • Sandwich bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter and jelly over a slice of bread.
  2. Add the second slice on top to complete the sandwich.
  3. Use a bowl or crimper to press on the sandwich to seal the edges.
  4. Peel the excess bread away.
  5. Freeze for two hours.
